Random Kart

This application is a totally unauthorized add-on for the very popular Mario Kart 8 by Nintendo for the Wii U™.

The app came about because my family plays Mario Kart for hours at a time. However, after a while, we discovered that each of us tended to favor the same character and kart combination.

So we started by throwing dice for each player, kart, tire, and wing combination. It took awhile, and it's hard to find 18-sided dice. We had to come up with something a little simpler. One day my son suggested that it would make a great mobile app. So I sat down and created this.

While I believe I have not infringed on any copyrights or violated any trademarks, there is always the potential that Nintendo could come in an shut this app down.
